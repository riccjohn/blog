My Raspberry Pi died, so I had to rebuild my home server from scratch.

While doing that, I finally got fed up with a problem I'd been quietly ignoring for months. Every tutorial I found told me to access my services at `my-domain-name.com/pihole`. None of them explained how to do that without exposing my home network to the internet or hosting it in the cloud.

I'd been reading past those gaps for years, assuming I was missing something obvious, but never having time to dive in. It turns out I was just missing the concepts that nobody thought to explain.

So I asked Claude to teach me how it all worked before we tried to build anything. That approach made a bigger difference than I expected. I wrote about it — link in the comments.
